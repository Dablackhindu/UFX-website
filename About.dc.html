UFX is a real-time technology studio. We capture physical spaces and rebuild them as living, navigable environments in Unreal Engine — starting with the industrial world that most people never get to see.

THE STUDIO

A new way to see the places that keep the world running.

We started UFX to replace the flat, stitched 360° tours corporate and industrial clients had settled for. Using XGRIDS reality capture and Gaussian Splatting, we reconstruct entire facilities as real-time environments in Unreal Engine — deployable to VR, web and any device.

Our breakthrough came at Casepak, a recycling facility in Leicester. We didn't just scan it — we told its story, pairing a precision digital twin with a content film that earned real industry traction. Industrial facilities are our wedge: complex, unglamorous, and long overdue for a way to be seen properly.
