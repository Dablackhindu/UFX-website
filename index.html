(03) — FEATURED CASE STUDY

CASEPAK
DIGITAL TWIN

DIGITAL TWIN · UNREAL ENGINE · 3DGS

A real-time, interactive digital twin of Casepak — a complex industrial recycling facility — rebuilt from over 200,000 drone and LiDAR images and deployed in Unreal Engine for training, maintenance and operational planning.

200K+
IMAGES CAPTURED
60+ FPS
REAL-TIME AT 4K
Sub-mm
ENGINEERING PRECISION
TB-scale
DATASET, OPTIMISED
CHALLENGE

Intricate machinery layouts and a ~200,000-image dataset demanded sub-millimetre accuracy. Traditional photogrammetry couldn't process or deploy it in real time.

TECHNOLOGY

Hybrid capture with the XGRIDS Portal Cam, Gaussian Splatting, and Volinga converting raw PLY into optimised NVOL assets — deployed natively in Unreal Engine.

RESULTS

A static dataset became a fully navigable real-time environment with live overlays for equipment specs, maintenance schedules and operational insights.
